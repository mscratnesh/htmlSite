Moving Average Conversion

📊 Overview

Moving Average Conversion with daily periods like 5, 10, 50, and 250 uses crossovers among these Simple Moving Averages (SMAs) to confirm trend shifts, filter noise, and time entries/exits in delivery-based stock trading on NSE.

📈 Standard Hierarchy & Roles

  • 5-day SMA: Short-term momentum; reacts fastest to price changes, ideal for entry triggers.
  • 10-day SMA: Near-term trend; confirms 5-day signals, reduces minor whipsaws.
  • 50-day SMA: Medium-term trend filter; acts as dynamic support/resistance (weekly equivalent).
  • 250-day SMA: Long-term trend baseline (~1-year); major bull/bear divider, rarely crossed.

Price above all = strong uptrend; below all = downtrend. Stacking aligns for confluence.

📝 Crossover Signals

  • Short-Term Buy (Golden Cross Minor): 5-SMA crosses above 10-SMA + both above 50-SMA. Enter delivery longs (e.g., RELIANCE at support).
  • Medium-Term Confirmation: 10-SMA > 50-SMA (while above 250-SMA). Scale in; hold positional.
  • Major Bull Shift: 50-SMA crosses above 250-SMA ("Big Golden Cross"). Strong buy for multi-month holds.
  • Sell Signals: Reverse crosses (e.g., 5<10, or 50<250 "Death Cross"). Exit or avoid.
  • DMA Twist: Displace shorter MAs +3-5 bars forward for earlier signals (e.g., 5-DMA +3 over 10-DMA).

💼 Trading Rules for Delivery

  • Entry: Bullish crossover (5>10>50, all >250) on volume >1.5x avg. Buy full qty for T+1.
  • Stop-Loss: Below 50-SMA or 2-3% from entry.
  • Target/Exit: Trail to 10-SMA or reverse cross; partial at 50-SMA resistance.
  • Filters: ADX>25 (trending); RSI 40-60 avoids extremes. Skip if 50<250 (bear filter).

Example: RELIANCE Daily — 5-SMA crosses 10-SMA at ₹2850 (above 50-SMA ₹2820, 250-SMA ₹2600) → Buy 100 shares. Hold to 50-SMA death cross at ₹3100 (+9%) or trail 10-SMA.
