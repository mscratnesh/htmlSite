📈 Technical Analysis

Technical analysis focuses on price action, chart patterns, and indicators to understand market trends and timing. It helps traders identify entry and exit points using tools like support/resistance, moving averages, RSI, and volume analysis.

Learn how to read charts, spot trend reversals, and build disciplined strategies for intraday, swing, and positional trading.

📊 Core Topics

  • Chart Patterns: Head & shoulders, triangles, flags, and breakouts.
  • Indicators: Moving averages, RSI, MACD, and volume indicators.
  • Support & Resistance: Identify key levels for entries and exits.
  • Trend Analysis: Detect trend direction and momentum shifts.
