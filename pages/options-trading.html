📈 Options Trading

Options trading gives you the right (not obligation) to buy or sell at a fixed price. It can be used for hedging or generating income with defined risk.

Learn calls, puts, premiums, and how option Greeks impact pricing and risk.

📊 Key Concepts

  • Calls & Puts: Directional rights to buy or sell.
  • Greeks: Delta, Gamma, Theta, Vega, Rho.
  • Strategies: Spreads, straddles, and hedges.
  • Risk Control: Defined loss vs. unlimited risk.
