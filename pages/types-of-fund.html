Types of Mutual Funds

📊 Types of Mutual Funds in India

India's mutual funds are professionally managed pooled investments regulated by SEBI, categorized into equity, debt, hybrid, solution-oriented, and other types with specific subtypes based on 2026 classifications.

Units are allotted at daily Net Asset Value (NAV); investors use lump sums or SIPs for diversified exposure to stocks, bonds, etc., with returns from capital gains or interest.
