⚠ Risk Management

Risk management protects your capital by defining how much you can lose per trade and per day. It is the foundation of consistent trading and investing success.

Learn how to set stop-losses, calculate position size, and manage risk/reward before entering any trade.

📈 Core Principles
  • Position Sizing: Risk a fixed % per trade.
  • Stop-Loss: Predefine exit to limit loss.
  • Risk/Reward: Target higher reward than risk.
  • Discipline: Follow rules consistently.
