0DTE Option Selling

📈 Overview

0DTE (Zero Days To Expiry) Option Selling is an intraday strategy that capitalizes on rapid time decay (theta) on expiry day. By selling far out-of-the-money options with premiums just above ?3, traders aim to capture quick profits as premiums decay to ?1, while maintaining strict risk controls with 200+ point buffer from spot.

  • Timing: Enter positions around 10:00 AM to capture peak theta decay hours.

📊 Re-entry Rules

  • Wait for Setup: Only re-enter when fresh strikes meet the ?3+ premium and 200-point criteria.

⚠ Risk Considerations

  • Tail Risk: Black swan events can cause extreme losses despite 200-point buffer.
  • Margin Requirements: Selling options requires substantial margin; ensure adequate capital.
  • Slippage Risk: Fast-moving markets on expiry can trigger SL at worse prices.
  • Position Sizing: Never risk more than 2% of capital per trade; limit total positions.
  • News Events: Avoid trading on major event days (RBI policy, budget, etc.).

📊 Objective

Capture rapid theta decay on expiry day while keeping tail risk controlled through wide strike selection and disciplined stop-loss execution.
