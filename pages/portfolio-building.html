📈 Portfolio Building

Portfolio building focuses on diversification, asset allocation, and long-term growth. It helps reduce risk while improving returns over time.

Learn how to balance equity, debt, and other assets based on your goals and risk profile.

📚 Building Blocks

  • Diversification: Spread risk across assets.
  • Allocation: Match investments to goals.
  • Rebalancing: Maintain target mix over time.
  • Review: Track and adjust periodically.

📈 Core & Satellite Portfolios

A core & satellite approach combines a stable core portfolio with smaller satellite positions for growth or tactical opportunities. The core typically holds diversified, long-term investments, while satellites target specific themes, sectors, or strategies.

  • Core: Broad-market funds or blue-chip holdings for stability.
  • Satellite: Sector funds, thematic ideas, or high-growth picks.
  • Allocation: Keep core larger (e.g., 70-80%) and satellites smaller.
