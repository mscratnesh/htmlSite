📊 Trading Strategies

Trading strategies help you plan entries, exits, and risk controls for different market conditions. Learn how to approach intraday, swing, and positional trades with discipline and clear rules.

This section focuses on strategy selection, setup identification, and practical execution so you can trade with confidence.

📝 Popular Approaches
  • Intraday: Fast trades based on momentum and volume.
  • Swing Trading: Multi-day trades using trend and support/resistance.
  • Positional: Longer-term trades with fundamental + technical alignment.
  • Risk Rules: Fixed stop-loss, position sizing, and risk/reward ratio.
📊 Strategy Highlights
  • Moving Average Conversion: Use crossovers to confirm trend shifts and entries.
  • 0DTE Option Selling: Same-day expiry selling with strict risk controls.
  • Monthly Income Option Selling: Systematic premium collection using defined setups.
  • Option Wheel: Cash-secured puts and covered calls for income.
  • Bori System (FIFO): Rule-based trade management using FIFO discipline.
