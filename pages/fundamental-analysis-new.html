📊 Fundamental Analysis

Fundamental analysis helps you evaluate a company's intrinsic value by studying financial statements, business performance, and economic factors. It supports long-term investing decisions by identifying strong, undervalued companies.

Learn how to read balance sheets, profit & loss statements, cash flows, and key ratios to compare companies and build a strong portfolio.

📈 Key Areas

  • Financial Ratios: P/E, P/B, ROE, and debt ratios.
  • Business Quality: Management, moat, and competitive position.
  • Growth Analysis: Revenue, profit, and cash flow trends.
  • Valuation: Compare price vs. intrinsic value.
